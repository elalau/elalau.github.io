From coloring books to sketchbooks, and now fiddling with graphic design programs, art has been a prominent part of my life for as long as I can remember. Being a pandemic high school graduate, my college experience has been quite different than a typical one. I entered college with a biology major (the pre-med to art track pipeline is real) but switched out my first semester after being locked in my room alone with my thoughts. Nonetheless, after switching back to in-person learning, I’m grateful to have found USF’s Advertising program. The Advertising program has a creative track that allows me to pursue my dreams of being a graphic designer while discovering the advertising world.

I’m very passionate about visual communication and the power it holds. I’m thrilled to be entering the advertising world through design.
